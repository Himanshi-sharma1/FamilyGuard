Your family cares about your safety

This app helps your family know you are safe. Please read what information is shared:

📍

Location Sharing

Your location will be shared with your family every 30 seconds so they can see where you are.

🎙️

Voice Recording

Background audio recording so your family can listen to your surroundings for your safety. You can turn this off anytime.

📷

Camera Access

Your family can request to see through your camera when needed for your safety. You will always be notified.

🚨

Emergency SOS

Press the SOS button anytime to immediately alert your family that you need help.

✅

Check-in System

Press "I Am Safe" button to let your family know you are okay.

💊

Health Tracking

Track your water intake, mood, and medications.
